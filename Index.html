Why the importance of Ukranian language is on time?

Language is an important object of emerging legal relations between citizens, organizations and authorities, since it is a tool for forming official, oral, written, informative messages, statements, appeals, contracts, etc. In addition, it is a social reality that requires special normative and legal regulation. The dynamism of language as a form of social consciousness and as a means of social communication, its multi-functionality allows us to talk about the existence of special linguistic relations that fall within the sphere of legal influence. The legal, and above all legislative, consolidation of the social functions of languages under the correct formulation of the question "allows to take into account the level of national and cultural self-awareness of the people, their social needs, and also creates conditions for neutralizing efforts of subjective interference in the processes of language functioning"

Executed Renaissance

For many centuries, the Ukrainian intelligentsia and ordinary people tried to destroy, bribe, deceive or hide from the world, but they could not. The "executed renaissance" became just another bloody period for haters of freedom and everything Ukrainian.The literary output of 1920s Soviet Ukraine has acquired almost legendary status in Ukrainian scholarship. It has been variously described as intense, creative, vibrant - exuding an aura of excitement and raw expressive power. Unparalleled, in quality and volume, by any other period in modern Ukrainian literature, the "executed renaissance" (rozstriliane vidrodzhennia), as it became known,' is seen as a national cultural flowering brutally crushed by the Stalinist repressions of the 1930s
